Obstacle Detection
A PiCamera feeds live video to the Navigation ESP32, which runs MobileNet SSD to classify obstacles — stairs, furniture, people, and more. When an obstacle is detected within 50 cm, motors stop immediately and clAIre speaks aloud: "Obstacle ahead — stairs at your left." The system operates at ~10 FPS continuously.
